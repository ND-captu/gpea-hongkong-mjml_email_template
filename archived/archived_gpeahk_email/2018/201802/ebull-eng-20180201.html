Without meaningful and urgent actions on climate change, the future of the Winter Olympics could be at risk. That’s why governments and corporations must do everything they can to break our addiction to fossil fuels now, before it’s too late.

There’s one company sponsoring this year’s Winter Olympics in PyeongChang, South Korea, which has the power to transform the global tech industry:

Samsung.

Samsung has a bigger energy footprint than a small country. But less than 1% of the energy it uses to make its products come from renewables, meaning that if it committed to 100% renewables, it would shift the industry and set a global benchmark for others to follow.

Samsung is still relying on dirty energy like coal to make our gadgets, directly contributing to climate change. For a company that spends USD14 billion a year on marketing, we are sure that Samsung can #DoBiggerThings and commit to 100% renewable energy!
