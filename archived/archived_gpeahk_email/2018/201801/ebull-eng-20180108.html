Month after month, you helped to protect our planet. Defend our oceans. Safeguard our forests. Change how businesses do business. Because of you, a whole lot of change happened in 2017. We can't thank you enough for being part of Greenpeace – and part of all this.

2017 has been a year of resistance, hope, and courage. If you have a minute, read about all this year's brightest moments:

See what we accomplished in 2017

If not we've listed 6 highlights below:

  • HSBC committed to stop financing forest destroyers after over 170,000 people called on the head of the bank to cut ties with deforestation.
  • A.S Watson Group and 759 stores announced they will aim to stop selling all rinse off products containing microplastics by the end of 2019.
  • In response to public pressure from the Rethink IT campaign, Samsung committed in February to refurbish its Note 7 instead of dumping 4.3 million phones with battery faults.
  • Thai Union, the largest canned tuna company in the world, agreed to end destructive fishing practices and improve its labour standards.
  • BP and Total had their applications to drill for oil near the Amazon Reef rejected by the Brazilian Government.
  • South Korea committed to phase out coal and nuclear power from their energy mix.

It is because of people like you that Greenpeace exists, we'd like to thank you for all the support! We hope this New Year brings you love, prosperity and strength because we still need you by our side.

Here's to another year of moving our world in the right direction together.
