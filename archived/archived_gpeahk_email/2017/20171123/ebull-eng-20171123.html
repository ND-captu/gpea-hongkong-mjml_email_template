Soft golden sands, blue lapping water – that’s what we dream of when going to the beach. But when Greenpeace visited 10 of Hong Kong’s more well-known coastlines recently, we were horrified.

The waterline was strewn with waste and most of it was single-use plastic such as straws, utensils and bottles. It was really distressing…

Greenpeace has always relied on scientific proof and so to assess the extent of the problem we are getting together with scientists to conduct a rigorous survey of marine plastic pollution around Hong Kong. And, guess who is coming to help? Our legendary ship, the Rainbow Warrior!

Although our hard-working crew will be super busy with this important task, they still really want to make time to meet you so we are excited to invite you on board the Rainbow Warrior for six open days on ship. Make sure you sign up today!
