Whatsapp, Facebook, Netflix, Google...Is your favourite app powered by dirty energy?

Every day millions of us connect with Netflix to stream endless hours of our favorite shows and films. In fact, ⅓ of all Internet traffic in the US is from Netflix. Right now Netflix and the data centres it relies upon run on dirty energy like coal. With the worst predicted impacts of climate change already happening this has to change.

We know this change is possible. Already, thousands of people like you and me made big companies like Apple, Google and Facebook listen and commit to renewable energy. Netflix has changed TV for the better. Now let's help them make the world better too and convince them to switch to 100% Renewable Energy!
