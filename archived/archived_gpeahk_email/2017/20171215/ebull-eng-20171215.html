After publishing Guide to Greener Electronics in October, we're stepping up to challenge Samsung Electronics, one of the largest smartphone manufacturers in the world and largest suppliers to others in the industry (yes it supplies key components to Apple, Oppo, Vivo, Xiaomi, Huawei and many others, even if you're not a Samsung fan...)

Championing the impossible? Doing bigger things? Talk is cheap, Samsung still relies on 19th-century dirty coal to make 21st-century gadgets.

How about renewable energy? ONLY 1%.

Thanks to your actions, tech leaders like Apple, Facebook & Google are taking actions to reach their commitment on 100% renewable energy. While boasting to be an innovation-driven, fast-moving and proactive global player, Samsung is way lagging behind to fulfill its vital role on reducing environmental impacts and climate change.

Samsung doesn't necessarily be the villain. All it needs to do is to stop fueling climate change and commit to 100% renewable energy in their production.
