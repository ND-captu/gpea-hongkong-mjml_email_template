What a win! Gore Fabrics, the maker of GORE-TEX® products and a major supplier of weatherproofing for outdoor gear, just announced at ISPO Munich that they will eliminate hazardous PFCs from their weatherproofing technologies.

How did we manage to convince Gore Fabrics to go free of hazardous PFCs without a public campaign?

You did it! You asked your favourite outdoor brands to stop polluting the environment and go PFC free. You signed petitions, took to social media, went to outdoor gear stores to say: "No more PFCs in our gear!"

Your favourite brands listened. Since the campaign started in 2015, many brands have issued statements confirming that they will eliminate PFCs from their products. This created enormous pressure on Gore Fabrics to provide them with weatherproofing solutions free of hazardous PFCs. And now Gore Fabrics have announced that they will.

You started a revolution in the outdoor gear sector but this victory is much bigger. You are truly amazing!

It is time for other industries using PFCs to follow this positive change. Here at Greenpeace, we will keep the pressure on all brands to make sure that they deliver on their promises.

This campaign victory belongs to YOU
