The discussion over the Lands Resumption Ordinance has been revived lately. This reminds us of our ongoing land-use issues and despite the rapid change in the political environment, sustainable city development is always on our agenda.
 
On Sept 25th evening, Greenpeace projected the calling to "Protect Lantau, Save Hong Kong", together with messages from the over 60,000 citizens who signed up for our "Protect the Lantau" campaign, onto the Government HQ at Tamar.
 
Greenpeace, together with you and all our supporters, will press on with our demand to the government for a full withdrawal of the $624 billion mega-reclamation under the Tomorrow Lantau Vision. We are asking the Government to protect Lantau waters from irreversible environmental damage, and instead go for the solution that costs 80% less - develop brownfields first!

More from Hong Kong
 
 
Dear Supermarket, please stop giving us this ridiculous plastic packaging! Families across Hong Kong are currently participating in a city-wide plastic packaging collection from supermarkets. A big thanks to those of you who have participated! Stay tuned for more on this activity soon. Meanwhile, we have launched our Go Plastic Free Pop-up store on wheels! It will stop in different districts from mid-September to mid-November. Come and experience plastic-free shopping first-hand, including loose fruits and veggies and a mini refill system!
