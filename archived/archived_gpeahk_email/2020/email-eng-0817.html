Proudly present to you the latest publication of "Annual Report 2019", with a deep appreciation of your support in the past 12 months.
 
2019 was a year of many challenges, environmentally and in many other aspects. Thanks for believing in us and standing with us in the face of climate emergency. Here’s a look back to what we've done together to make our world a better and safer place.
 
This report will also show you how we maximise your trust to push for green solutions in multiple ways. To name a few, investigating environmental crimes, conducting scientific research and publishing reports, education, engaging community participation to promote green living, and promoting environmental protection through media. There has been continuous dialogue with the governments and enterprises, lobbying and other advocacy work, to protect the environment forward.
 
The theme of this year annual report is #StrongerTogether. We believe solidarity brings hope, with hope and action, we can make positive changes. Hope you enjoy what we together have archived and looking forward to building more climate hope together going forward.
 
Thank you,
Greenpeace East Asia
