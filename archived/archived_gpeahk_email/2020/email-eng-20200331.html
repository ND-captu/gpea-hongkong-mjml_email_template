From Our Offices Worldwide
 
 
Good News! South Korea just became the first Asian country whose ruling party announced a climate manifesto, also known as the Korean Green New Deal. This manifesto announced on Mar16th includes a series of policies including net-zero emissions by 2050. The night before, Greenpeace's climate suffrage campaign team in Korea projected a message on the side of a large building, calling for the ruling party to take drastic measures against the climate crisis.
 
💪 Greenpeace Seoul welcomes the announcement but strongly recommends a feasible plan including a clear roadmap to achieve net zero emissions by 2050.
