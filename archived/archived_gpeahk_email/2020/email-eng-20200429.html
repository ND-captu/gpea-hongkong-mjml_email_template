Firstly, wish you and all your loved ones all safe and well. Even though the virus situation seems to be easing, let's not forget the lessons we learned from the pandemic: renewing our bonds and relationships with one another.
 
On Apr 22nd, we celebrated the 50th anniversary of Earth Day by launching a 5-day environmental film screening on our Facebook (in Chinese). The headline photo on top is a capture of the Earth-lovers, like you and me, who joined the opening screening forum. Thank you to all who participated and our special guests Daphne Wong (Director of “Breathing Room”, a documentary on Hong Kong's pink dolphins), Leung Wing-mo (Former Assistant Director of the Hong Kong Observatory, and local climate activist Lance Lau.
 
Perhaps this Earth Day shows us like never before how much the earth matters to each of us, and how deeply rooted we are in nature. Perhaps it gives us hope: revealing how impactful each of us can be in changing the course of our planet if we act for the greater good.
 
Thank you for loving the Earth and your support to Greenpeace. Together, let's decide now to create a world that puts wellbeing for people and the planet first.
