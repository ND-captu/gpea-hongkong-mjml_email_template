After months of campaigning and people powered actions around the world, Samsung has finally committed to our demands to not dump the 4.3 million Galaxy Note 7 recalled worldwide, but instead recycle them!

This is because of YOU! People power has once again proven to drive change!

Samsung also announced it will participate in new research led by the European Union aimed at developing a new environmentally friendly technology to recycle smartphones.

We at Greenpeace will continue to monitor and make sure Samsung takes into account the voice of millions of our supporters and abides by its commitment.

It's time our gadgets are as innovative for the planet as they are for our lives. We will soon score smartphone brands to see just how repairable they are - and we'll continue tracking how companies like Samsung live up to their promises. Stay tuned!
