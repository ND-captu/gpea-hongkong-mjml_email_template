It's summer and there's nothing better than a long, cool drink. But what do you think happens to the plastic cup and straw that we just used?

Too many end up in the oceans, where they can last for hundreds of years. Plastic debris maims or kills seabirds, turtles and fish (did you see that heart-breaking video of a turtle in terrible pain with a plastic straw in his nose?).

Can you believe that worldwide, the equivalent of a rubbish truck-full of plastic waste is dumped into the oceans every minute (or a whopping 12.7 million tonnes every year)?

Things have to change. And this is the summer we really DO something about plastics!

Sign the Plastic-free pledge Know more about the problem with plastic

The good news is there are many solutions out there and they all lead us towards a plastic-free future. For example, plastics can be replaced with stainless steel straws, reusable bottles and eco-wrap. We are pushing hard for governments and corporations to change but YOU can also make a huge difference. Your first step is to make a sincere pledge to switch to easy alternatives to plastic in your daily life.

And then of course cut down on using plastic as much as you can in your daily life. There are lots of good examples to follow -- countries and cities around the world have started banning single-use plastic -- France, for example, has banned disposable plastic plates and cutlery.

Now, it's time for all of us to stop condemning our oceans to a thousand years of pollution for just a few minutes convenience. This is the summer we really DO something about plastics? Are you with us? Come and join our plastic-free future!
