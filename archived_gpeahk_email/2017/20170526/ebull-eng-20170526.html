Being silenced by a corporate bully would be devastating and the scary thing is, this could really happen.

After years of Greenpeace successful campaigns to protect the Great Northern Forest a 26 million acre forestland across northeastern U.S, one logging company, Resolute Forest Products, is trying to silence Greenpeace and our ally Stand.earth by suing us for hundreds of millions of dollars.

If they win, Greenpeace's ability to advocate for forest protection anywhere in the world will take a huge blow.

We need support from Resolute's own customers. Many major publishing companies -- like Penguin Random House, HarperCollins, Simon & Schuster, and Hachette -- source paper from Resolute.

Can you ask the publishing industry to support Greenpeace and stand up for free speech? Your voice will help us convince publishers to stand up for free speech and the forests.
