Global Climate Action March 15 ! Support Hong Kong's Youth4Climate!
 
We care for the Earth. We together have been pushing for more Climate Action. Coming Friday, March 15, seize our chance to join the Global Climate Strike action and give full support to the students of our city and all around the world.
