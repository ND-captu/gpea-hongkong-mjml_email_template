ðŸ’šGoing Plastic-Free on Earth Day and beyond
 
Two weeks prior to Earth Day (Apr 22), adults and school children joined us as volunteers to go hunting for plastic-free shops in Shatin. The efforts paid off. On April 13th & 19th alone, our Plastic-Free Ambassadors included nearly 100 shops into the Shatin plastic-free community, meaning they are cutting down on single-use plastic ware or providing benefits to customers if they shop with their own carrier bags or containers.
 
On Apr 22nd, we invited some of the young Plastic-Free Ambassadors to a ceremony at our Earth Day event and presented them each with a Plastic-Free Ambassadors badge. Hats off to all our high-spirited ambassadors! (check out this Greenpeace Hong Kong fb video, Chinese subtitled).
