Samsung作為全球最大企業之一
卻停留在採用1％的可再生能源

全球氣候變化已成現實，現在我們必須積極行動，阻止全球最大企業之一 Samsung背道而馳，進一步加劇氣候變化。

雖然Samsung於媒體界宣傳「Championing The Impossible」和「Doing Bigger Things」的價值觀，但他們仍一直採用骯髒、舊式的化石燃料如煤炭。當APPLE已承諾採用100％的可再生能源生產的時候，Samsung仍停留在僅採用1％的可再生能源。

Samsung就如沉睡的巨人，我們絕不能坐視不理。Samsung必須擺脫化石燃料的枷鎖，聆聽並積極配合全球對保護地球和未來的聲音。

結合全球包括你我的聲音與力量，推動Samsung停止採用骯髒化石燃料的舊式工業時代，邁向使用可再生、潔淨能源的二十一世紀，守護地球的未來。

Dear Sirs,
您好，

I write to you today because we are running out of time. Climate scientists have proven that the health of our planet is at grave risk from climate change. Indeed, we are already seeing the consequences, from extreme weather to rising temperatures. In order to prevent irreparable damage to our communities, environment, and the economy, we must urgently transition away from dirty energy sources to renewable energy.
今天我寫這封信給您，因為我們已沒有時間可以浪費。氣候專家已經證明，地球正面臨巨大危機，也就是氣候變化。我們已經看到從極端氣候到全球暖化的後果。為了防止對我們的社區、環境和經濟造成不可挽回的破壞，必須盡快將骯髒能源轉換到可再生能源。

Samsung Electronics has positioned itself as a market leader driven by innovation but unfortunately when it comes to renewable energy your company is lagging far behind. Given Samsung’s huge global reach and influence in the sector your company can play a pivotal role in helping us transition away from fossil fuels as fast as possible.
Samsung將自己定位為創新驅動的市場領導者，但不幸的是，Samsung在可再生能源方面遠遠落後。有鑑於Samsung在這個領域的全球影響力，貴公司可以發揮關鍵作用，協助盡快由化石燃料淘汰轉型。

Around the world more and more progressive companies, politicians and people are realising the need for urgent action and working together to ensure we avoid the worst consequences of climate change.
全球越來越多進步的公司、政治人物和公眾，都意識到需要積極採取行動、共同努力，確保我們不會遭遇氣候變化的最壞後果。

Greenpeace has challenged the biggest technology companies to commit to renewable energy, and nearly twenty companies including Apple, Facebook and Google have already done so. Right now Samsung Electronics has the opportunity to join the leaders taking action on behalf of our planet by committing to 100% renewable energy.
綠色和平已經向最大科技公司呼籲，盡快承諾使用可再生能源，將近20家公司，包括APPLE、FACEBOOK和GOOGLE都已公開承諾。現在，Samsung是時候加入成為領導者，承諾100％的可再生能源。

I am writing on behalf of present and future generations to ask you, in your new leadership role at Samsung Electronics, to pioneer an exciting, new, forward thinking, clean and sustainable vision for your company. This is a chance to join other leading CEOs in committing their business to work towards creating a truly innovative, greener tomorrow. 我代表我們和未來世代提出要求，在您於Samsung電子擔任新的領導角色時，開創一個激動人心、全新、前衛、乾淨和可持續的願景。請您也加入其他CEO，致力創造一個真正創新、更環保的明天。
