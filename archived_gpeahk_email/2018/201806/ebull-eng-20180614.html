Over the last nine months hundreds of thousands of people around the world have stood up and sent a clear message to Samsung Electronics to live up to its innovative reputation and make our smartphones with renewable energy, moving away from the dirty energy like coal that contribute to global warming.

This proves that together, we can #DoBiggerThings!
