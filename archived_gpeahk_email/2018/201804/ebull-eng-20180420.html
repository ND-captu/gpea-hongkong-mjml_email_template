Weather is freezing and sea is roaring (Well, not the coldest season in the Antarctic yet), but they couldn't stop Greenpeace activists confronting a krill fishing vessel in Antarctic waters with a peaceful protest, making it unable to resume catching krill.

For TINY species like antarctic krill, they're BIG players in the antarctic food web, main food source for whales and penguins. However, Greenpeace's recent investigation revealed that the growing krill-fishing industry poses threats of food competition and environmental risks to antarctic species through intensive fishing near protected areas.
