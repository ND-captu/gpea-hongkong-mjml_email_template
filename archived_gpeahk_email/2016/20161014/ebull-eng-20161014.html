The world has finally taken a big step forward in the fight against dangerous global warming.

The Paris Agreement on climate change - the first universal, legally binding, agreement to cut carbon emissions - was voted into law by enough nations to come into force.

As we write this, extreme weather events made worse by an increasingly unstable climate are destroying lives around the world. Haiti has just experienced the destruction of Hurricane Matthew - the human cost still unknown. Our thoughts are with all of those who are experiencing the terror of climate disasters.

Stopping the climate crisis could't be more urgent.The "Paris Agreement" is a long way from perfect - but it's definitely a turning point.
